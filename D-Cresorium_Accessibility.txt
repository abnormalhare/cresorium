All Nouns are spelled with a capital letter. (Besides words that are shown with all lowercase)

SOV: Subject - Object - Verb

Normal English sentences use SVO:   I eat food
Cresorium sentences look like this: I food eat


Sentence Structure:
Noun (x) Subject--Verb Verb (x) Preposition:
English:   (I think) (commas are changing) (in the near future)
Cresorium: (I (commas are changing) think) (in the near future)

Questions:
Switch the verb to the beginning of the sentence. (VSO)
English:   (Why) (are; (you) ;running)?
Cresorium: (Running) (why) (you)?
